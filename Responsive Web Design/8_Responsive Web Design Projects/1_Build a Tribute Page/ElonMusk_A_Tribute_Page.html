About Him

  • Early Life - Elon Reeve Musk June 28 1971, (age 50) Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa

  • Education - In 1995 University of Pennsylvania, Graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in physics and a Bachelor of Science degree in economics.

    • Business Carrer

  • Zip2 - 1995 Founded Web Software Zip2 with others, Compaq acquired Zip2 for $307 million in cash in February 1999, and Musk received $22 million for his 7-percent share.

  • X.com - 1999 Musk co-founded X.com, renamed PayPal in 2001. In 2002, PayPal was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion in stock, of which Musk—the largest shareholder with 11.72% of shares—received $175.8 million.

  • SpaceX - In 2002 Musk founded Space Exploration Technologies Corp., traded as SpaceX.

  • Tesla - In 2004 he invested $6.5 million, became the majority shareholder, and joined Tesla's board of directors as chairman, Musk become CEO and product architect in 2008.

  • SolarCity and Tesla Energy - Tesla acquired SolarCity for over $2 billion in 2016 and merged it with its battery energy storage products division to create Tesla Energy.

  • Neuralink - In 2016, Musk co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology startup company to integrate the human brain with artificial intelligence (AI).

  • The Boring Company - In 2016, Musk founded The Boring Company to construct tunnels.
